FAQs

Does Quell-Ai disclose that it’s an AI during calls?

Yes. Every call begins with a transparent introduction so callers know the assistant is AI-powered, aligning with emerging regulations.

Can I control which calls the AI handles?

Absolutely. Use the important contacts whitelist and per-number rules to decide when the AI steps in.

How is my data protected?

Instructions, transcripts, and recordings auto-expire by default, live in encrypted storage, and can be deleted anytime.

Does it support texts and other messaging apps?

Yes. Quell-Ai auto-replies to SMS with your tone, filters spam keywords, and is designed to expand into popular messaging platforms.
